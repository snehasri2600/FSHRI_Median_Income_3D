## Inspiration - Herwig Scherabon

<br>

This dashboard was inspired by the work of Herwig Scherabon, an Austrian architect-turned-data artist currently based in Berlin, Germany. His work involves creating 3D visualizations, whether in structural form, motion pictures, or digital experiences.  

<img src="Herwig_Scherabon_001-4-1.jpg" alt="Herwig Scherabon" width="250">

<br>
<br>

Scherabon's work often explores themes of urbanism, environment, and social equity, using data to reveal hidden patterns and narratives within cities. My favorite visualizations of his involved 3D representations of inequality in different cities.
